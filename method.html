Overall Merhod Implemented by Me

1) Selection of participants

I sent out a survey form asking people if they played chess and if they would like to be part of my research, I then selected people who were willing to participate. Each category had 4 people.(Link to the survey in the appendix)

2) Puzzle selection

I selected a series of logic grid puzzles, maze puzzles and riddles I then asked each participant to complete the puzzles while I timed them.

3) Accuracy and Time

I measured the time it took for each participant to complete the maze puzzles and recored the accuracy and their problem solving apporaches to each question.

4)Data Collection

I recorded the data I collected in a word document and then analysed it to see patterns and find out the statistics.

5) Comparision of Data

I compared the data to see if the chess players performed better than the non-chess players.

6) Website creation

I compiled all of my research into this website.
