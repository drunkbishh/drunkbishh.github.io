Analysisng the numbers

Mean time for the easy maze puzzles

= Time taken by all the participants / number of participants in the group

Chess players: 50/4 = 12.5 seconds

Non-chess players: 67/4 = 16.75 seconds

12.5 > 16.75, Therefore the chess players are better than the non-chess players at the easy maze puzzle

Mean time for the medium maze puzzles

= Time taken by all the participants / number of participants in the group

Chess players: 157 / 4 = 39.25 seconds
Non-chess players: 178 / 4 = 44.5 seconds
